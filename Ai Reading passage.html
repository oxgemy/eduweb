Technology in Our Daily Life

In this lesson, you will read a short text about AI technology and answer questions. Use the steps to help your reading.

Before you read

  • Prediction: Look at the picture. What might the text say about AI?
  • Skimming: Read the first and last sentences quickly. What is the main idea?
  • Scanning: Find two benefits of AI. Find one risk or problem.
  • Prior knowledge: Where do you see AI in your life?

Reading

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is a kind of computer technology. It helps machines learn from data. AI can work fast and make simple decisions. Many people use AI every day. It helps us search the internet and choose music. It can also help doctors look at images and find problems. In schools, AI can give practice and feedback to students. In offices, it can do boring tasks and save time. AI is not perfect. Sometimes it makes mistakes. People must check the results and use it carefully. AI should be fair and safe for everyone. With good rules and smart use, AI can make life easier for many people.
